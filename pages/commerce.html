Explore Careers in Commerce

Discover a wide range of career opportunities in the fields of business, finance, economics, and entrepreneurship.

Accounting

Accountants prepare and analyze financial records, ensuring accuracy and compliance with laws and regulations. They work in areas such as auditing, taxation, and corporate finance.

Business Management

Business managers oversee the daily operations of organizations, make strategic decisions, and ensure that business goals are achieved efficiently and profitably.

Finance

Careers in finance involve managing investments, assets, and capital for individuals or organizations. This field includes financial analysis, portfolio management, and investment banking.

Marketing

Marketers develop strategies to promote products and services to target audiences. They use market research, branding, digital marketing, and advertising to create demand and increase sales.

Human Resource Management

HR professionals manage the recruitment, training, development, and welfare of employees. They ensure that companies adhere to labor laws and maintain a healthy work environment.

Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurs create and manage businesses, identifying new opportunities and innovations. They take risks to develop products and services that meet market needs and drive economic growth.

Economics

Economists study the production and distribution of resources, goods, and services. They analyze data to understand economic trends and provide insights for policy-making and business strategy.

Banking

Bankers work in institutions that offer financial services such as loans, savings, investments, and wealth management. They guide individuals and companies in managing their financial portfolios.

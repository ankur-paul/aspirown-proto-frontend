Software Engineer

Details

Career Overview

Software engineers design, develop, and maintain software systems. They work on everything from applications to operating systems, ensuring the programs are efficient and reliable.

Salary & Job Opportunities

Average Salary: $80,000 - $150,000 per year

Job Growth: Excellent (projected to grow 22% by 2030)

Key Skills

  • Problem Solving
  • Coding (Python, Java, C++)
  • Teamwork and Communication
  • Critical Thinking

Key Subjects to Focus On

  • Mathematics (Algebra, Calculus)
  • Computer Science
  • Physics
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
